There is a fountain filled with blood

1. PARUTIVI rwoM’ponisi wangu

Pakapopoma rwizi rweropa,

Vese vatadzi vanosamba’po

Vachibuda vati mbuu kuchena.


2. Mbavha yakafa paMuchinjiko

Yakashambidzwa muurwo rwizi

Neni ndisina pakati mbee,

Ndingati mbuu muna’rwo rukova.



3. Ndizvo basi ndosimbawo nazvo

Ndabva mumhaka isingaperi,

Hapachinazve nerimwe zita

Rinondifadza kupinda rake.



4. Kana ndichinge ndowokotsira

Rurimi rwangu rwanyararisa

Ndinodudza norurimi rutsva,

Ndinodudza norurimi rutsva,

Muponisi wakandinunura.
