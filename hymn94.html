94

When God of old came down from Heaven

8888

1. KARE Mwari wakadzika

PaSinai nesimba;

Makore akatsemuka

Nomwoto neutsi.


2. PaPendekost wakauya

Nesimba norudo;

Njiva yake yakadzika

Nokuzvirereka.


3. Mwoto pamusoro peSinai

Wakatyisa kwazvo

Zvino unojeka kwazvo

Pavatsvene vese.


4. VaIsraeri vakanzwa

Izwi rine ruzha,

Rainge tsuri yaMwari

Yakabva kudenga.


5. Panguvayo izvo Mweya

Wakauya pasi,

Ruzha rukuru rwemhepo

Rwakabva kudenga,


6. Chechi yakazadzwa zvino

Nepasi resezve,

Vane mwoyo mikukutu

Havachanzwi Mweya.
