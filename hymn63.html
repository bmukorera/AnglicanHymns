HYMNS

63

Ride on, ride on in majesty

8888

1. FAMBA muumambo hwako!

Uri pambongoro yako;

Vanhu vese vachiimba

Nemichindwi nezviimbo.


2. Famba muumambo hwako!

Kupfumvu, nhamo norufo!

Kristu mbiri yavambira

Kukunda guva neshungu.



3. Famba muumambo hwako!

Ngerosi dzese dzedenga

Dziri kusuva nyamusi

Ngepfungwa yomuteuro.



4. Famba muumambo hwako!

Dambudziko razosvika;

Baba pachigaro chake

Ari kumirira Mwana.



5. Famba muumambo hwako!

Kupfumvu, nhamo norufu!

Masure mezvo simuka

Utonge mumbiri yako.
