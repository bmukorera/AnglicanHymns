HYMNS

73

Forgive them, O my Father

8888

1. JESU wakataura

Achiroverwa mbambo;

Baba vasunungure

Ngekuti havazivi.


2. Jesu haasakatuka

Kana akaroverwa,

Wakavanamatira

Achida Baba wake.

3. Kuneni mutsa wake,

Kuneni rudo rwake,

Neniwo ndaenzana

Navakamurovera.



4. Zvitadzo zvangu zvese

Nembambo dzakaipa,

Zvakarovera Tenzi

Zvakanga zviri zvangu.




5. Kazhinji ndarishora

Izwiro rine mutsa,

Ndisunungure, Jesu,

Ndatadza kwazvo ini.




6. Nomutsa wako, Jesu,

Norudo rwako, Tenzi,

Dzorera vakarasika

Ngekuti havazivi.
