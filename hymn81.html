HYMNS

81

Christ the Lord is risen to-day

8888

1. NGEKUMUKA kwake

Kristu wakakurira,

Pasi raponiswa

Ndiye Mambo wedu;

Hondo dzengerosi

Dziri kufarira,

Dzamuvirikidza,

Dzaimba Areruya.


2. Uyo wakazvipa

Kare paMâ€™chinjiko,

Ndiye Paska yedu

Nyenye yakachena;

Wakafira isu,

Zvino ane mbiri,

Tese tinofara,

Tiimbe Areruya.


3. Uyo wakafira

Isu vanhu vake,

Zvino muponisi

Anesimba rese;

Mambo wavamambo

Anotonga vese,

Ngatimutunhidze

Tiimbe Areruya.


4. Wakatidzorera

Varasiki vese,

Vangasunungurwe

Vanyangadzi ndiye;

Paska yedu iwe

Tichenurwe tese,

Tikukudze Kristu;

Tiimbe Areruya.
