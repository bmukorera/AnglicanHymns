SAMU

SAMU 10

1 UNOMIRIREI kure, Tenzi : uchisvisa hope yako panguva yatinokuda tiri kutambudzika?


2. Wakaipa pamusana poruchiva rwake anotambudza varombo : ngavabatwe mukunyangadza nomumazano avakafunga.


3. Nokuti wakaipa kwazvo wakazvikudza noruchiva rwomoyo wake : nokutaura zvakanaka navanochiva, avo Mwari anovenga.


4. Wakaipa anodada kwazvo, ndizvo haakatari ndiMwari : haafungi ndiMwari mupfungwa dzake dzese.

5. Nzira dzake dzakaipa nguva dzese : mitemo yako iri kure naye, ndizvozvo anoshora kana nemhandu dzake dzese.


6. Nokuti wakati mumoyo wake, Ha, handizokurirwi : hakuna chakaipa chinoitika kwandiri.


7. Muromo wake uzere nokutuka, nokunyengedza nokunyepa : pasi porurimi rwake panokuipa nokunyangara.


8. Anozvisvisa munzvimbo dzembavha dzakavandika mumigwagwa yomumisha : nokuvandika mumapako ake anouraya vasina mhosva; maziso ake haane tsitsi kuna varombo.


9. Nokuti anorindira nokuvandika, seshumba mubako rayo : kuti amanikidze murombo nesimba


10. Anomanikidza murombo : kana amubata mumambure ake


11. Anoputsika pasi achizvidukupisa : kuti sangano ravarombo ribatwe ngevatariri vake


12. Wakati mumoyo make, Ha, Mwari wakanganwa : anogara akaringisa rutivi, haambozvioni.


13. SIMUKA, Tenzi Mwari, usimudze ruoko rwako : usakanganwe varombo.


14. Munyangadzi anotukirei Mwari : achiti mumoyo make, Ha, iwe Mwari haukatari ndizvo.


15. Chokwadi iwe wakazviona : nokuti iwe uoona kuipa nokusarurama.


16. Kuti iwe umudzivirire mumhosva : murombo anozvipa kwauri; nokuti iwe ndiwe mubatsiri wavasina shamwari


17. Paradza simba romunyangadzi naye anemafi : bvisa kuipa kwake kubve kwapera.


18. TENZI ndiMwari narini : navahedeni vapera munyika.


19. Tenzi, wakanzwa zvinoda varombo : unogadzirira moyo yavo nenzeve dzako dzinozviteerera;


20. Kubatsira vasina vababa navarombo kuti vaitirwe zvakafanira : kuti munhu wepasi asavatyisezve
