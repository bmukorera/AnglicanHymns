HYMNS

Sing, my tongue, the glorious battle

8888

1 IMBIRAI ndimi dzese

Kurwa kune mbiri,

Mese tunhidzai Kristu,

Ndiye Mukuriri

Muteuro paM’chinjiko,

Wakatiponisa.



2. Nguva yake yapfuura,

Yokugara nesu,

Muponisi anozvipa

Kune nhamo zhinji.

Nyenye yaroverwa zvino

Mupereko wedi.




3. Mbambo, pfumo wakorera

Minzwa nokupfirwa;

Muviri wakadurura

Mvura neRopawo.

Tsime tsvene rokugeza

Vanhu nezvisikwa.

4. Iwe Muchinjiko kwawo

Muti une mbiri;

Une ruva nomuchero

Zvinopinda zvese;

Uyo apo aturikwa

Ndiye Musikiwo.



5. Kombamisa mhandu dzako,

Muti’yo murefu;

Kuoma kwako kwekare

Ngakupfave zvino;

Ndiwe uri kutakura

Mambo wekudenga.



6. Iwe wega wakafanira

Kutsigira Tenzi;

Ndiwe vanhu vagashira

Ruponiso rwavo;

Nyenye yavazora zvino

NgeRopa rinokosha.



7. Mbiri kuna Mwari Baba,

Kuno Mukorore,

Kuno Mweya Unoyera

Mumwe ngeVatatu,

Mwari ane simba rese

Agere narini. Amen.
