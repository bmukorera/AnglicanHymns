Thy Lamb’s high banquet we await

8888

1. TADAIDZWA kusangana

Pano paChidyo cheNyenye;

Takabira nyanza Tsvuku

Tiimbire Jesu Mambo.


2. PaOtari yoM’chinjiko

Wakatiripira mhosva;

Ropa rake ngatirimwe,

Tigare naye ndiMwari.


3. Vanhu vakasunungurwa

Muhasha dzaMambo Faro;

Vachiponiswa usiku

Pakapfuura ngerosi.


4. M’ponisi wakabaiwa,

. SeNyenye isina vara;

Nyama yake, chingwa chedi,

Inondopiwa kunesu.



5. Muteuro unokwana,

Wakakurira Gehrna;

Vasungwi vasunungurwa,

Vapiwa upenyu ndiye.


6. Tiri kukudza rumuko;

Rwakauyazve nesimba,

Rukasunga mhandu yedu,

Nokutiwanira musha.


7. Mbiri ngaive kunewe.

Wakamukazve mukufa,

Mbiri kuna Mwari Baba,

Nokuna Mweya Mutsvene. Amen.
