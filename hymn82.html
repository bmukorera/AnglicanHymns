HYMNS

82

At the Lamb’s high feast we sing

8888

1. TINOVIRIKIDZA

Mambo wakurira,

Wakarasa ropa

Saka takachena;

Kristu muteuro

Kristu mupiristi

Muri chidyo chedu,

Tasvusvudzwa ndiye.


2. Kudya kwedu, kwedi

Muviri waKritu;

Pano tinorimwa

Ropa rinoyera;

Ngativirikidze

Kristu Paska ydu,

Mana yokudenga

Chingwa choupenyu.


3. Kare vaipona

Vanhu na’ro ropa,

Vakabira nyanza

Mhandu dzakanyudzwa;

Nhasi taimbira

Jesu Muponisi;

Ndiye Nyenyedzi yedu

Yakafira isu.


4. Tenzi Muteuro

Wakakunda kufa

Wakatinunura,

Wakavura Denga;

Mbiri kunaKristu,

Ndiye Muponisi

Tinomutunhidza

NaBaba noMweya. Amen.
