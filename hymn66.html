HYMNS

8888

1 PO Jesu akaroverwa

Pakati pavatadzi

Gororo paMuchinjiko

Rakati kuna Jesu

Zvibatsirewo iwe

Uponise nesuwo.


2. Imwe mbavha yopindura,

“Rega tatadza tose;

Uyu haana kutadza;

Ndimudzidzisi wedu;

Rega kumbomutuka

Munhu mutsvene uyu.



3. Jesu ishe ndakuteta

Munditonderewo

M’ushe hwenyu hwokudenga

Apo munosvikamo.”

Jesu akapindura,

Ndichakugashiramo.



4. Nhasi uchazova neni

Muparadesi umo;

Kana tafa pamwe chete

Tichazogara umo;

Vese vanopinduka

Vanopinda kudenga.
