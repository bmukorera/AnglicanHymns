HYMNS

86

On the resurrection morning

8888

1. MUSI uyo wokumuka

Mweya unouyazve;

Nhamo kufa nokuchema,

Zvapera.

2. Asi mweya wabva pano

Muviri wozorora,

Uchirara mumakuva

Muhope.


3. Muvirizve wakaneta

Unorara muguva,

Kumirira musi uyo

Koedza


4. Asi mweya chisimbirira

Kunamata Mwari,

Uchivirikidza Mwari

Norwiyo.


5. Mweya zvese nomuviri,

Hazvizopatsaniswi,

Kuyenzana naye Kristu

Zvagutswa.


6. Rufaro rwomusi uyo

Wokumuka mukufa,

Haruzoperi narini

Narini.


7. Baba, mai, hama, vana,

Pamusi worumuko,

Vachazokumbanazve norufaro.


8. Pakuguma tichasvika

Kuupenyu hwembiri,

Nokugara newe uko

Kudenga.
