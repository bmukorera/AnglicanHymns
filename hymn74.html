HYMNS

74

At the Cross he station dkeeping

8888

1 Mai anosuruvara,

Anomira achichema,

Petyo poMchinjiko

Mwoyo wake wakatombwa

Nepfumo rinipinzisa

Rorusuvo norudo




2. Mai wakasuva sei!

Panguva yedambudziko

RoMukorore wake.

Mai wakatendeseka

Wakamuchemera sei!

Muponisi wepasi.




3. Ndiani asingagoni

Kuchema, kuona mai

Achijakwacha apo?

Ndiani, achitondera,

Kusuva kwamai wacho

Asingakukoveri?




4. Wakaona Tenzi Jesu

Kukorera Muchinjiko,

Pamusana pezvitadzo.

Wakamirira, dakara

Akakotama musoro

Nokuregera mweya




5 Mai worudo nenyasha

Ngandinzwe kusuwakwako

Kuti ndicheme newe.

Rudo rwaKristu runotsva

Mumwoyo wangu narini,

Kuti ndimufarire.



6 Ndipe kuti nditakure

Mavanga aTenzi Jesu,

Mumuviri wanguwo;

Ndipe kuti ndimwe ropa

Rakaparadzirwa isu,

PaMuchinjiko wake.




7 Panguva yokufa kwangu

Ngeminamato yaMai

Ndidzivirire, Tenzi

Ndipe kuti mweya wangu,

Ugarewo mukupenya

Kwepasi rinouya
