HYMNS

69

There is a green hill far away

8888

1. PAGOMO kunze kwemusha weJerusarema

Jesu wakaroverwa kutiponisa tose.

2. Hatizivi nhamo’yo akatambudzikira

Asi tese tinoziva, wakafira isu.

3. Wakafira kutiita tirurame tese,

Kutikokera kudenga ngeM’chinjiko wake.

4. Hapana munhu angasungure zvitadzo,

NdiJesu ega wakatizururira denga.

5. Wakatidisa, nesu tese ngatimudewo,

Tisimbire muropa rake nokumufadza.
